Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2–3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour eggs into the pan and cook evenly.
  4. Add fillings when eggs begin to set.
  5. Fold the omelette and cook another minute.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy.
